What We Done Last Year

Miracle Primary School

Last year saw further development done to the toilet blocks at Miracle Primary School. We also shared the word of God and played games and activities with the children, including and whole school sports day.

The Medical Mission

The Medical Mission continued to be a massive success in not only the community of _____, but also ______. Hundreds of people were given sanitary and medical aid, as well as clothes. On top of that we also had a fun/sports day with the kids!

Outreach

We ventured out to prostitutes, drug and alcohol abusers, and prisoners, giving them word of how they can change their lives for the better and that there is still hope in the world for them. We started a seed-funding for the prostitutes to start businesses of their own.
